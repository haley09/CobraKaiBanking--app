The Accounts We Offer

Checking Account

A checking account is designed for everyday money management. It gives users quick access to funds for regular activity such as purchases, transfers, and withdrawals. In Cobra Kai Banking, checking accounts represent convenience, flexibility, and day-to-day financial use.

Checking accounts are best for users who want easy access to their balance and frequent account activity without locking away their funds.

Savings Account

A savings account is designed for storing money over time and supporting long-term financial goals. In Cobra Kai Banking, savings accounts represent growth, financial planning, and interest-based account behavior.

Savings accounts are best for users who want to build reserves, prepare for emergencies, or better understand how balance growth and interest work in a banking system.

Why Account Types Matter

Different account types help demonstrate how financial products can serve different purposes. This simulation uses account types to show how banking systems organize account rules, withdrawal limits, and interest behavior.
